Privacy Policy

Welcome to SquishDash. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

SquishDash does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The game operates entirely offline on your device.

The only data stored is your local highscore, which remains on your device using SharedPreferences and is never sent to any server.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data usage beyond the local game state stored on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

SquishDash does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. There are no in-app purchases or external connections.

4. Children's Privacy

SquishDash is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults. The game contains no user accounts or data collection of any kind.

5. Data Security

All game data (highscores) is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data and it is protected by your device's own security measures.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in app updates. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all local game data at any time by using the "Clear Scores" option in the Highscores screen, or by uninstalling the app.
